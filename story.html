Magic Candy: A Story About Sharing

Start Middle End

Mario Loves Candy

Mario is a little boy who loves sweets. His favorite? Candy!

The Magical Jar

Today, Mom gives Mario a magical-looking candy jar.

Mom says, “This is magic candy. You can have one piece a day!”

First Taste

Mario takes a bite. It’s the sweetest candy he’s ever had!

His eyes lit up. “Yum! This is amazing!”

Ann Wants Some

Ann sees the candy and asks, “Can I have one too?”

Mario frowns, “But... I only have one piece.”

Not So Happy

He doesn’t feel as happy as he thought he would, even after eating the candy.

The candy was sweet, but something felt missing.

The Jar Speaks

The jar suddenly speaks, “Sharing candy brings even more magic!”

Mario gasps, “Really?”

Sharing Time

Mario breaks his candy in half and gives some to Ann.

Ann smiles, “Thank you, Mario!”

More Candy!

Mario finds three new candies in the jar!

The magic was real!

Fun Together

Everyone is laughing, sharing, and having fun together.

Mario says, “Sharing feels even better than eating it all by myself!”

Happy Ending

The jar glows softly, as if saying goodnight.

From then on, one candy a day brought lots of joy and friendship.
